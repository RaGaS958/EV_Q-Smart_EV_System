About EV_Q:?

The world's most comprehensive multi-agent EV intelligence platform

🎯 Our Mission

EV_Q democratises electric vehicle knowledge by combining cutting-edge multi-agent AI with real-time web intelligence. Whether you're a first-time buyer, a current owner needing support, or a fleet manager analysing costs — EV_Q is your always-on expert team.

🔧

Technical Expertise

Expert diagnosis for battery issues, charging faults, range anxiety & maintenance with step-by-step guidance.

🚗

Market Intelligence

Compare models, track live pricing, estimate resale values & check real inventory via web search.

💳

Financial Clarity

True cost of EV ownership — EMI breakdowns, subsidy maximisation & comprehensive TCO analysis.

🛡️

Policy Navigation

Navigate insurance, warranties, safety compliance & government incentive programmes with confidence.

🧠

Smart Recommendations

Personalised EV picks ranked by your unique budget, usage pattern & charging access.

⚡

Real-time Data

Live Google Search integration — every agent has access to the latest market and technical information.
